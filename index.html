About Me

 

"It is the ultimate luxury to combine passion and contribution. It's also a very clear path to happiness."
- Sheryl Sandberg

Born and bred in the Boston suburbs, I traveled all the way to western Massachusetts to study journalism and marketing at the University of Massachusetts Amherst. With a dream of becoming the next Ira Glass, I interned at WBUR, NEPR and a small start-up radio show called PassWord while I studied abroad in London. Then, I took an Introduction to Web Design class, and helped Professor McDermott turn it into UMass' first MOOC. I realized I had a knack for this website stuff and decided to pursue it further. In 2014, I graduated from UMass and took a job as a Web Content Specialist at the Massachusetts Department of Higher Education. In this position I have fine-tuned my web design skills and worked on projects that promote public higher education and help students access and succeed in college.

A Change in UMass Housing Causes a Stir

You'd think seniors would have seniority when it comes to housing on the UMass campus. But starting next year, that won't be the case.
